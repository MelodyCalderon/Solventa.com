About Us

Already a leader in the continent’s southern cone segment, with more than 20 years of traditional financial experience, Solventa has migrated to the Fintech industry in Argentina, Colombia, Perú and the United States.

We help and encourage people to face unexpected expenses without fear, through immediate financing and a simple process.

Our main goal is to offer better alternatives to people who are joining the growing Fintech ecosystem, providing greater possibilities to finance unexpected expenses.

When there are economic emergencies to solve, Solventa, with few requirements, is the best option.
